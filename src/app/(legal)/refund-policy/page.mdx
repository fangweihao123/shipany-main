# **Refund Policy for Nano Banana**

## **Overview**

At Nano Banana, we stand behind our AI image generation services and strive to ensure customer satisfaction. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions under which you may request a refund for your purchased credits.

---

## **Refund Eligibility**

To be eligible for a refund, your request must meet the following criteria:

### **Time Limit**
Refund requests must be submitted within **7 days** of your original purchase date.

### **Usage Limit** 
You must have used **less than 50%** of your purchased credits at the time of the refund request.

### **Valid Reason**
You must provide a clear reason for requesting the refund.

---

## **How to Request a Refund**

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

### **Step 1: Contact Our Support Team**
- **Email**: [support@ai-nanobanana.net](mailto:support@ai-nanobanana.net)
- Include your **order number** and the **reason** for your refund request
- Provide any relevant details about your experience with our service

### **Step 2: Review Process**
- Our team will review your request within **2-3 business days**
- We may contact you for additional information if needed
- You will receive an email notification regarding the status of your request

### **Step 3: Refund Processing**
- If approved, refunds will be processed within **5-7 business days**
- Refunds will be credited back to your **original payment method**
- You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed

## **What Happens After a Refund**

- Your account credits will be deducted to reflect the refunded amount
- Access to premium features may be restricted if your credit balance falls below the required threshold
- Any generated images or content created with the refunded credits will remain accessible to you

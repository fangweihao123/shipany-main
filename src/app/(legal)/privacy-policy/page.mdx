# Privacy Policy for SoVideo

_Effective date: January 1, 2025_

## Introduction

Welcome to **SoVideo**, the cinematic video creation platform powered by the Sora2 API. We help teams turn text prompts and reference images into production-ready video. Protecting your privacy is central to how we operate. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when interacting with SoVideo.

## Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to deliver and improve the SoVideo experience:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What we collect**: Name, email address, password (hashed), and any profile details you choose to share.
   - **Why we collect it**: To create and secure your account, provide customer support, and communicate service updates.

2. **Prompts, Assets & Generated Media**

   - **What we collect**: Text prompts, negative prompts, reference images, seed values, and the video renders produced through the Sora2 API.
   - **Why we collect it**: To process your generation requests, display render history, troubleshoot issues, and help you manage outputs within your account.
   - **How we handle it**: Uploaded assets and generated clips are stored securely and, unless you opt to archive them, are automatically purged on a rolling basis consistent with your plan settings.

3. **Usage & Diagnostics**

   - **What we collect**: API usage metrics, render settings, timestamps, feature interactions, and event logs.
   - **Why we collect it**: To monitor platform performance, optimize render quality, enforce fair-use policies, and develop new features.

4. **Device & Technical Data**

   - **What we collect**: Browser type, device identifiers, operating system, IP address, and session metadata.
   - **Why we collect it**: To secure your account, detect abuse, and deliver a consistent experience across devices.

5. **Cookies & Similar Technologies**

   - **What we collect**: Session cookies, preference cookies, and analytics tags.
   - **Why we collect it**: To keep you signed in, remember settings such as locale or theme, and understand product usage trends. You can manage cookies through your browser controls.

6. **Billing Information**

   - **What we collect**: Payment method details, billing address, tax information, and transaction history (processed via PCI-compliant providers).
   - **Why we collect it**: To activate paid plans, prevent fraud, and comply with financial regulations. We never store full card numbers on SoVideo servers.

## How We Use Your Information

We process personal data to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the SoVideo platform and APIs
- Tailor the user experience and recommend workflows or templates
- Offer customer support and communicate about service changes
- Enforce terms, prevent fraud, and ensure platform security
- Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

We do **not** sell your personal data.

## Sharing Your Information

We share data only in limited situations:

- **Service providers**: Trusted partners who help us host infrastructure, process payments, store media, or send communications. These partners are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.
- **Legal requirements**: When required by law, court order, or government request, or to defend our legal rights.
- **Business transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, provided the acquiring entity honors this policy or notifies you of material changes.

## Data Security

We employ encryption, access controls, network monitoring, and routine audits to safeguard your data. While no system is perfectly secure, we continuously improve our safeguards and encourage you to enable multi-factor authentication and use strong passwords.

## International Data Transfers

SoVideo operates globally. When data is transferred across borders, we rely on recognized legal mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses and ensure comparable protections regardless of where data is processed.

## Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access, correct, or delete your personal information
- Export a copy of your generation history
- Object to or restrict certain processing activities
- Update cookie preferences or opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at **privacy@sovideo.ai**. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

## Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Rendered media and uploads follow the retention rules of your subscription plan. When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

## Children's Privacy

SoVideo is not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a minor has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. When we make material updates, we will notify you via email or an in-app message and update the effective date above. Continued use of SoVideo after the changes take effect signifies acceptance of the revised policy.

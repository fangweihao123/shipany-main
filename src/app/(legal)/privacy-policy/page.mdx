# Privacy Policy for Nano Banana

## Introduction

Welcome to Nano Banana, the advanced **AI Image Generation Platform** designed to identify AI-generated images and protect against fake content. At Nano Banana, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using Nano Banana:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account and provide customer support.

2. **Image Data**

   - **What We Collect**: Images you upload for AI generation analysis. Images are processed in real-time and immediately deleted after analysis.
   - **Purpose**: To provide AI image generation services and analysis results.

3. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use Nano Banana, including generation requests, API usage, and feature interactions.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve generation accuracy, and enhance our services.

4. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access Nano Banana, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility.

5. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services and personalize your experience.

6. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods, such as credit card number, billing address, and any details required for transaction processing.
   - **Purpose**: To facilitate billing and payment processing for our services.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. The data we collect is stored securely on our servers, and we implement a variety of security measures including encryption and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. **Important: Uploaded images are processed in real-time for AI generation and are immediately deleted after analysis - we never store your images.** However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.
